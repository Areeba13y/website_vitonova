Founder's Vision Founder's Vision

Ghulam Murtaza

Founder & CEO

Welcome to VitaNova International Alliance for Sciences! I'm Ghulam Murtaza, and I feel proud to lead this phenomenal organization, empowering individuals to excel in the world of sciences by supporting progressive research, collaborations, and specialized scholarship guidance sessions.

This platform is designed with passion and purpose. As someone who understands the challenges and ambitions of exploring a career in life sciences, I founded this initiative to provide personalized guidance and resources to empower your dreams.

Whether you're seeking support in research or exploring collaborations, attending our scholarship guidance sessions, or planning your way to study abroad, our mission is to make every step of your journey meaningful and achievable.

"Your journey matters, and we're here to guide it."

— Ghulam Murtaza

Our Mission Our Mission

VitaNova International Alliance for Sciences (VNIAS) is an international non-profit scientific council dedicated to supporting science students and researchers in life sciences and IT fields.
