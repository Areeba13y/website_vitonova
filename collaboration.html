Global Collaborations

Building bridges for scientific excellence and sustainable development

United Nations Economic and Social Council

Partnership for Sustainable Development

The collaboration between VitaNova International Alliance for Sciences (VNIAS) and the United Nations Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC) aims to advance global sustainable development through joint initiatives in research, education, and community outreach. It focuses on research and virtual events in plant sciences, climate change, biodiversity conservation, and food security; career development for underprivileged students; international scholarships and exchange programs; access to e-learning platforms; AI-based skill enhancement workshops in life sciences; and ambassador programs promoting the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Together, both organizations strive to empower youth, foster innovation, and build a more inclusive and sustainable future.

Minister of Foreign Affairs - USA: Dr. Alberto Flores Hernandez

Shaheed Benazir Bhutto University

Shaheed Benazir Abad, Sindh, Pakistan

This collaboration aims to advance integrated life sciences by co-hosting remote conferences and research initiatives in plant sciences, climate change and biodiversity conservation. It focuses on developing innovative projects in plant bioinformatics and computational biology, providing training and development opportunities and supporting international scholarships and exchange programs. The partnership will also offer university students access to advanced e-learning platforms, enhance skills through AI-integrated life sciences workshops, promote collaborative academic writing and publications and launch community outreach and ambassadorship programs at Shaheed Benazir Bhutto University, Sindh, in alignment with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Vice Chancellor: Eng. Prof. Dr. Madad Ali Shah

POSITIVE PLUS FOUNDATION

Empowering Communities

VitaNova International Alliance for Sciences is proud to collaborate with POSITIVE PLUS FOUNDATION, a dedicated organization committed to empowering individuals through education, research, and skill development. This partnership aims to provide life sciences students and researchers, especially from developing countries, with greater access to international training workshops, seminars, and mentorship programs. By combining our expertise in computational biology and bioinformatics with POSITIVE PLUS FOUNDATION’s mission of fostering academic and professional growth, we strive to create impactful learning opportunities. Together, we are working towards building a stronger scientific community by promoting knowledge exchange, leadership development, and career advancement for aspiring researchers worldwide.

Founder: Dr. Hossain-Al-Amin

Co-founder and Vice President: Dr. Maidah

Protection of Natural Resources (PNR)

Mutual Collaboration in Educational Initiatives

This Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) sets forth the terms and understanding between VNIAS and PNR Protection of Natural Resources or mutual collaboration in educational initiatives, training programs (International conferences, Seminar, webinars, internships and short courses) and research activities aimed at empowering students of developing countries in the field of life sciences as well as other science domains with a special focus on guiding them toward securing international scholarships and enhancing academic profiles.

CEO: Ms. Arooj Fatima

African Youth Network

Empowering Young Leaders

VitaNova International Alliance for Sciences is honored to collaborate with the African Youth Network, an organization dedicated to empowering young leaders across Africa through education, mentorship, and skill development. This partnership focuses on providing life sciences students and researchers with access to international training programs, research opportunities, and leadership development initiatives. By working together, we aim to bridge the gap between education and professional growth, equipping young scientists with the necessary skills to drive innovation and scientific progress in their communities. Through this collaboration, we are committed to fostering a dynamic and inclusive scientific ecosystem that empowers the next generation of African researchers and changemakers.

Founder & President: Amb. Emmanuel Ferdinand

MONARCHS MATTER

Youth-led Conservation

As a youth-led organization, Monarchs Matter believes in the power of young voices and communities to drive positive change. We organize family workshops, coordinate international educational webinars, showcase youth stories on the blog, and host the annual Raise, Tag, and Release (RTR) Summer Program. Through a hands-on, learning-while-doing approach, the RTR Program teaches students the importance of biodiversity and native species—all while they raise and tag monarch butterflies, collect citizen science data, and restore native habitats. By empowering youth through education and action, we are committed to inspiring our next generation of environmental leaders and conservationists.

Founder and President: Cynthia Zhang

UF GenoForge

Biotechnology & Bioinformatics

VitaNova International Alliance for Sciences is proud to collaborate with UF GenoForge, a biotechnology and bioinformatics company dedicated to advancing scientific research and education. This partnership aims to strengthen cooperation in biotechnology, bioinformatics, and academic development, providing students and researchers with valuable opportunities for training, mentorship, and knowledge exchange. Through joint workshops, webinars, and conferences, we seek to support skill-building initiatives and help the next generation of scientists grow. We are working towards creating a more connected and innovative scientific community by combining UF GenoForge’s expertise in cutting-edge research with VitaNova International Alliance for Sciences’ commitment to scientific collaboration. Together, we are committed to expanding research opportunities, promoting academic growth, and empowering young scientists to make a meaningful impact in life sciences.

Director & CEO: Ayesha Farooq
